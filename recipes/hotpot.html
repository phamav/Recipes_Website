Hotpot Recipe

Description

This hotpot recipe is perfect for a cozy dinner with family and friends. It features a flavorful broth packed with a variety of ingredients, such as tender meat, fresh vegetables, and tasty noodles. The hotpot is a communal dish where everyone can gather around and cook their preferred ingredients in the bubbling broth. It's a delightful and interactive dining experience!

Ingredients

  • 1 liter chicken or vegetable broth
  • 500g thinly sliced beef or pork
  • 200g firm tofu, cut into cubes
  • 200g shiitake mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 bunch of spinach, trimmed
  • 200g udon noodles
  • 4 green onions, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Pour the chicken or vegetable broth into a hotpot or a large pot and bring it to a simmer.
  2. Add the sliced beef or pork, tofu, shiitake mushrooms, and spinach to the simmering broth. Let them cook for a few minutes until they are tender.
  3. Cook the udon noodles according to the package instructions, then drain them and set aside.
  4. In a small bowl, mix together the soy sauce, minced garlic, and sesame oil.
  5. Once the ingredients in the hotpot are cooked, season the broth with salt and pepper to taste. You can also adjust the seasoning by adding more soy sauce if desired.
  6. Divide the cooked udon noodles into serving bowls. Ladle the hotpot broth and ingredients over the noodles.
  7. Garnish each bowl with chopped green onions.
  8. Serve the hotpot immediately and enjoy!
